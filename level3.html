Narrative Context

How it Works

You will read a research artifact that contains hidden flaws. These may include unsupported claims, ethical violations, or incorrect interpretations of results.

Your mouse is your red pen. Click only when you are confident an error exists.

If you are right, you gain XP. If you are wrong, you fall into a trap.

Educational Deep Dive (Strategy)

  • Language: Watch for overgeneralization
  • Ethics: Check informed consent and withdrawal rights
  • Numbers: Claims must match statistical significance

Your Task

Audit the document carefully. Click only on real flaws. Ignore the traps.
